Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for how trash valet businesses actually operate — not a generic field service tool with a new coat of paint.

🏢
Property Management
Organize all your apartment complexes. Store manager contacts, unit counts, contract rates, and access notes for every property.
👥
Customer Profiles
Every customer gets a full profile — address, unit, phone, email, service plan, billing cycle, pickup days, and special instructions.
🗺️
Daily Route View
See exactly who's on your route today. One tap to mark done, one tap to skip. Real-time progress as you work.
👷
Driver Management
Add every driver. Assign customers and properties to specific drivers. Filter today's route by driver instantly.
📊
Completion Reports
Generate professional reports for property managers. Show exactly what was done, when, and by whom. Download in seconds.
💰
Revenue Tracking
Monthly recurring revenue, annual projection, and a full billing breakdown by plan and customer — always accurate.
📱
Built for Mobile
Designed iPhone-first. Large tap targets, bottom navigation for one-handed use, full-screen forms, no zoom on input focus.
🗓️
Weekly Schedule
See all 6 days at once with every customer under their pickup day. Tap any stop to mark it done instantly.
🔐
Multi-Account
Every operator gets their own isolated account. Multiple people can share a device — data never bleeds between accounts.
