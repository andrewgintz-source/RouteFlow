Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for how trash valet businesses actually operate — not a generic field service tool with a new coat of paint.

🏢
Property Management
Organize all your apartment complexes in one place. Store manager contacts, unit counts, contract rates, and access notes for every property.
👥
Customer Profiles
Every customer gets a full profile — address, unit number, phone, email, service plan, billing cycle, pickup days, and special instructions.
🗺️
Daily Route View
See exactly who's on your route today. One tap to mark done, one tap to skip. Progress bar shows your completion in real time as you work.
👷
Driver Management
Add every driver on your team. Assign customers and properties to specific drivers. Filter today's route by driver to see who's responsible for what.
📊
Completion Reports
Generate professional completion reports for every property. Show your property managers exactly what was done, when, and by whom. Download and send instantly.
💰
Revenue Tracking
See your monthly recurring revenue, annual projection, and a full billing breakdown by plan and customer. Know exactly what you're owed every month.
📱
Built for Mobile
Designed for iPhone first. Everything works perfectly on your phone — full-screen modals, tap-friendly buttons, and bottom navigation built for one-handed use on route.
🗓️
Weekly Schedule View
See all 6 days at once with every customer under their pickup day. Tap any stop to toggle it done. Spot gaps in your schedule instantly.
🔐
Separate Accounts
Every user gets their own isolated account. Multiple operators can share a device — each signs in to their own data. Nothing bleeds between accounts.
